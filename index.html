Internet Explorer Mode Detection

Have you ever been in a situation where a client is complaining about how their site is rendering in IE9 or 10? Ugh. More times than not, the browser is running in forced compatibility mode. While you could walk them through the steps to help troubleshoot the issue, you can confirm it easily by downloading and connecting to the following script:

<script src="http://www.yoursite.com/js/ie-mode/detect.js"></script>

Here you will be presented with the following message when changing the browser mode via IEs Developer Tool.

You are viewing this site in Internet Explorer 8 or Internet Explorer Compatibility Mode. You should consider upgrading your browser or speaking to your IT department about dropping support for older browsers.
